About Us

Philippians 2:3-4 says, "Do nothing from selfishness or empty conceit, but with humility of mind let each of you regard one another as more important than himself; do not merely look out for your own personal interests, but also for the interests of others." We believe in this commission to serve and felt it vital to create a platform of service that would have a lasting impact on the lives of today's young men and women.



Many student athletes today come to college ill equipped to deal with balancing athletics and scholarship and are often unaware of the financial issues involved with being an NCAA scholarship athlete. The SMART Program addresses these issues with classroom instruction that assures the student athlete has an understanding of what will make them a success in life.



The SMART Program captures our heart and vision to see student athletes trained to be financially empowered for post college life, whether they become professional athletes or enter other fields of endeavor. For this reason, we are committed to taking the SMART Program and its resources to student athletes nationwide.



Our vision will be achieved by focusing on the difficult problems facing young adults and by working with our partners to create innovative solutions. To that end, with our dedication and your support, we feel confident the SMART Program will change the future of this generation by providing young adults with the tools needed to live healthy financial lifestyles. Join us as we impact change one student athlete at a time.



In Pursuit of Excellence,





J.P. Musgrove and Rebecca Musgrove

Co-Founders
