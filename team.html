Board of Directors


J.P. Musgrove, Co-Founder and President,

leads the program's efforts to provide financial literacy to collegiate student athletes nationwide. He sets strategic priorities, monitors results, and facilitates relationships with each of the program's key partners.



Directly after college, J.P. worked as an auditor for Burr, Pilger & Mayer, Inc. of San Francisco and later joined the Financial Services Division of MassMutual Financial Group. J.P. is a member of Kaleo Christian Fellowship of Oakland and is actively involved in the men's ministry and the children's Sunday school program. J.P. currently serves as an NCAA Life Skills Speaker and Alumni Committee Member at St. Mary's College of California.



J.P. was born in Los Angeles, California. He grew up in Pasadena, California and graduated from La Salle High School as an all-conference and all-state varsity letterman. J.P. and his wife Rebecca currently reside in Concord, California.



Jerome Lansangan, Treasurer and General Counsel,

oversees Finance and Accounting, and assists in working to advance the program's mission.



Jerome received a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with an emphasis in Accounting from California State University, East Bay.



Currently, Jerome is an Assurance Supervisor at Burr Pilger Mayer, Inc.. He has over five years of experience in public accounting serving public, private, governmental and non-profit organizations in a wide variety of industries including high-tech, financial services, educational institutions, and life sciences. He is also actively involved as a Catechist at St. Clement Church in Hayward, California.



Christian C. Sherk, Secretary and General Counsel,

provides organizational guidance and creative solutions for the program's expansion and growth.



Christian C. Sherk, Secretary and General Counsel, provides organizational guidance and creative solutions for the program's expansion and growth.



Chris is the current owner of two small businesses and has, within the context of several business partnerships, established and sold nine small businesses over the past 25 years. In addition to his business ownerships, Chris has also been the owner of rental properties in the Oregon area.



Chris was born in Pasadena, California and grew up in Sierra Madre, California. He graduated from Pasadena High School and attended two years of college at Pepperdine University. Chris and his wife Trudi Sherk of 34 years, have raised three daughters and are expecting their first grandchild in 2011. They currently attend Cedar Mill Bible Church in Beaverton, Oregon and have been long-time supporters of multiple missionaries and churches.
