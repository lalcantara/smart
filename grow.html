Help Us Grow

We feel the need for financial literacy, particularly among student athletes, is greater than ever. The SMART Program's operations rely heavily on contributions from foundations, corporations, and individuals. We need your financial partnership to help ensure the expansion and growth of the SMART Program nationwide.

Continuous Improvement

The SMART Program is committed in an ongoing effort to improve the quality of its products and services. We are ambitiously seeking innovative ways to increase our instructional approach and enrich the value of our content. This dedication to improvement will keep our information relevant and ensure that our student athletes graduate with the tools needed for financial success


Educational Outreach

Benjamin Franklin said, "Without continual growth and progress, such words as improvement, achievement, and success have no meaning." To that end, we desire continual growth and outreach to define our success as an organization. We will create periodic editions of DVD curriculum and textbooks that will multiply the impact of SMART's outreach to student athletes nationwide.


SMART Program Headquarters

The SMART Program is seeking to purchase office space in the San Francisco Bay Area that will serve as its headquarters to provide adequate space to run our programs and to facilitate growth.
